Dynatrace Monitoring:
	Azure Native Dynatrace Service (ANDS) --> SaaS solution
	Available through Marketplace
	Deployed in Subscription --> Resource Group --> Region
	Shows up as single resource --> No Managed RG --> No dependent resources

	Sending traffic --> Sending to Marketplace Partners

	Sending traffic --> Network Egress
		Depends on Region of deployment
